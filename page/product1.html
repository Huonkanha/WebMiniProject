Blue Book 2024: Tiffany Céleste
Our newest high jewelry collection is a mesmerizing journey through the cosmos, inspired by legendary Tiffany & Co. designer Jean Schlumberger. Reimagining his iconic celestial motifs, each piece juxtaposes custom-cut diamonds and colored gemstones with unique shapes and visions.
By the Number
The Wings necklace was crafted over a span of 1,732 hours and set with a D-color, internally flawless oval diamond of over 20 carats, four trillion-cut diamonds of over 2 total carats, 131 custom-cut baguette diamonds of over 14 total carats and 1,841 round brilliant diamonds of over 58 total carats.
Extraordinary Savoir Faire
Archival Jean Schlumberger designs, including his Plumes necklace, transform into stunning new creations showcasing extraordinary craftsmanship, as well as striking diamonds and unenhanced sapphires. The miraculously crafted designs within the chapter feature intricate applied gold details through the use of 18k yellow gold wirework, a hallmark of Jean Schlumberger design.
Soaring Creativity
Symbolizing flight and fantasy, the designer’s iconic wing silhouettes transform into a luminous story rendered in diamonds and unenhanced sapphires. Handcrafted from individual castings in a pattern reminiscent of celestial bodies, the Wings pendant is a cosmic take on the wing motif.
“This chapter evokes a divine ethereal spirit and features a masterpiece necklace, one of the most important and exceptional pieces within the collection.”
